Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 5, 2025

Happenings is a mobile app developed to help users discover, post, and attend local events in the Greater Seattle area. Your privacy is important to us. This policy outlines the types of information we may collect and how we use and protect it.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: We may collect your email address or username if you create an account or post an event.
  • Location Data: With your permission, we use your device’s location to show nearby events. This data is not stored or shared.
  • Event Content: Any content you post, such as event details or images, is stored securely and only shown within the app.
  • Device Info: We collect basic device information (e.g., Android version, screen size) to improve app performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To personalize and improve your event discovery experience
  • To allow you to post or attend events
  • To ensure app functionality and user safety

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties. Data may be shared only when required by law or to comply with Google Play policies.

4. Children's Privacy

Happenings is intended for users ages 12 and up. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 12. If you believe we have done so in error, please contact us immediately.

5. Security

We implement reasonable safeguards to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Any changes will be posted here with a revised date.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please email us at: happeningsapp@gmail.com
